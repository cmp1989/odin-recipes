Lasagna

Description:

Special homemade lasagna recipe with sauce that you make from scratch. Best served with salad and garlic bread.

Ingredients:

    1/2 lb ground pork and 1/2 lb lean ground beef 1/2 cup minced onion 1 can tomato sauce (28 oz) 1 can crushed tomatoes (8 oz) 2 tbsp fresh parsley divided 1 clove garlic crushed A dash of sugar 1.5 tsp dried basil 0.5 tsp dried oregano 1.5 tbsp salt and 2 tsp salt 1/8 tsp white sugar 1/4 tsp ground black pepper 3 large eggs 1 (16 oz) pack of lasagana noodles 1 lb small-curd cottage cheese 1 (16 oz) package of shredded mozzarella cheese
